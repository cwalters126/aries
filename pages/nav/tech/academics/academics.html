CS4500: Database Design and Implementation

I taught an in-person, 400-level undergraduate course on Database Design and Implementation at Aurora University during the fall 2021 semester. I created course materials, including lecture slides, and assignments, in order to make the course engaging and useful. The course focused on teaching students how to design, create, and manipulate relational databases using SQL. The course concluded with a semester-long project where students designed and implemented their own database system.
